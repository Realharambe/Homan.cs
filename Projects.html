BUNKER

Bunker is a solo project of my made in unity 6 that im currently working on, it is a slow burn pyschological horro game taking place entirly in a bunker you're alone in a smallish bunker maybe 4ish rooms following a appocolypse/end of the world but as your doing your daily routine of waking up eating and checing on the systems and staying by the radio station your water turns off so you goto fix it but over the coming days spanning probably a week and a half more stuff starts to fail more frequantly and during that time theirs random sound events like knocking on the hatch and stuff rustling around in the vents and sometimes you hear whispering and muffled talking over the radio and theres some mini/random events like investigate the sound in the vents etc, and when looking down the hallway or looking near a doorway you'll see a shadowy figure reflecting the characters mental state and what the isolation has done to them or is the figure actually there?
